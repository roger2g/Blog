THE HEALTH BENEFITS OF THE TRAIL

TRAILS

Would you like to breathe the fresh mountain air, feel the pulsating energy of nature, listen to the sound of birds and get your body moving? If so, this post is for you!

Nowadays, people are increasingly closed in offices and in contact with computers and cell phones. We end up forgetting how important it is to move the body after all, our body is programmed for physical activities.

Most people don't like to exercise and avoid sports as much as possible. However, there are so many options for them that, with affection and desire, the person will certainly be able to find something that amuses and causes, at the same time, pleasure.

Hiking is a sport that increasingly attracts new lovers. It is a wonderful option because it includes entire families, skinny, chubby, elderly and children. In other words, it is democratic!

Much more than described above, the practice of trails brings numerous health benefits, follow with us some of them:

WELFARE

The simple fact of being in contact with nature already calms our mind and the breathing rhythm is already altered. Hiking releases hormones responsible for our well-being and happiness, such as dopamine and serotonin.
Through this sport, depression and bad mood are avoided. A blessing!

CONTACT WITH NATURE

The current world of big cities makes us increasingly disconnected from the great Mother Nature. Cars, pollution, noise, technology and speed take center stage.
All of this together culminates in chronic stress and anxiety.

When hiking, the fresh air, the symphony of nature and the landscape make us reconnect with the natural essence of things, making us lighter and less stimulated. Calm comes to meet us and the very organic rhythm of things creates a new meaning.

FISIC BODY

The constant practice of trails can provide weight loss, high blood pressure control, muscle strengthening and flexibility.

In a paradisiacal environment, the Hotel, in addition to offering extreme comfort and unique cuisine , offers hiking trails in its range of entertainment, in safe and beautiful places, where the whole family can benefit from this practice.

The trails are complete because they bring benefits not only to the physical body, but to balanced emotions, to harmonize thoughts (mental control), to re-energize with nature and, of course, leave the physical body full of vitality.

How about taking advantage of the holidays to gather the whole family for days of health and leisure ?
