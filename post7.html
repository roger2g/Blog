THE BENEFITS OF FRESH AIR MOUNTAINS FOR HUMAN HEALTH

PURE MOUNTAIN AIR: HEALTH BENEFITS

Air is one of the essential elements for life. But for human beings to enjoy good health, it is essential that the air they breathe is of good quality. Among the many benefits of clean air are:

  • Helps prevent respiratory and circulatory problems;
  • Increases quality of life and well-being;
  • Helps prevent eye, nose and throat irritation;
  • Increases life expectancy;

TAKE A DEEP BREATH

Breathing clean air is crucial for good health. Air contains approximately 20% oxygen, with the remainder being nitrogen and other gases. Since the human body runs on oxygen, each of the cells needs to receive a constant supply of this element. Generally, air pollution acts as a supporting factor in the illness of people with respiratory and cardiovascular diseases; therefore, it is important to spend as much time as possible in contact with nature, where the air is cleaner.

FRESH AIR

The air is cooler in the morning. It helps to oxygenate the lungs and benefits blood circulation, as it is the time with the lowest concentration of impurities in the air. Internal environments such as bedrooms, living rooms and other rooms should always have windows open or between open to ensure good air circulation and keep them airy, especially at night. This prevents the accumulation of dust and makes the environment more suitable, ensuring well-being. Good breathing is the one that allows the sensation of the lungs opening and the air flowing through them, thus ensuring the restoration of the organs and nervous system.
