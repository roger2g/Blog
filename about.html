ABOUT OUR BLOG

The blog was created with the aim of connecting nature more with people, to show them that nature is essential for our daily lives. So the preservation of the environment is essential to maintain the health of the planet and all the living beings that live on it.

OUR MISSSION

Our mission and make readers aware to lead an active life with nature, show them that we need the environment, and we need to preserve it to avoid the worst. Have you ever imagined the world without the green color of trees, of the most varied colors and species of flowers, have you ever stopped to think what the world would be like without trees, without nature? So this is our mission to make people aware of having a healthy life and caring for nature.
