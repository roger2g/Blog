BENEFITS OF MOUNTAINING FOR HEALTH

WHAT IS MOUNTAINEERING?

First it is important to understand what mountaineering is, shall we?

Basically, it is a sport and leisure practice that aims to climb mountains and rocky elevations, either through hiking or climbing. In addition, mountaineering has different degrees of difficulty and duration. Another interesting point is that mountaineering covers some activities, such as:

  • Hiking in the mountains;
  • High mountain;
  • Rock climbing;
  • Ice and snow climbing;
  • Climbing artificial walls

BENEFITS OF MOUNTAINEERING

Once you start mountaineering it is impossible to stop. Do you know why? We can spend hours looking at the green of the forests, the gray of the rocks and admiring every detail of nature.

In addition, activity is good for health and that's what we're going to talk about now! Read on and check out five health and life benefits of mountaineering.

INCREASE YOUR ENVIRONMENTAL AWARENESS

The first and one of the main benefits of mountaineering is the increase in your environmental awareness, that is, from the moment you start in the sport and have direct contact with nature, your posture changes. It happens naturally and you start to develop a new look at the environment, adopt attitudes of care, appreciation and preservation.

IMPROVED ABILITY TO CONCENTRATE

The practice of mountaineering requires the development of your awareness in the present, attention to your actions and everything around you. Therefore, to be successful in sport, it is important to have concentration and be committed to yourself (physically and emotionally) and to nature. With the constancy in mountaineering, the improvement of the ability to concentrate and focus becomes part of your daily life. So, another one of the benefits of mountaineering for your personal and professional life.

ALLOWS YOU TO MEET PEOPLE AND MAKE NEW FRIENDS

Of all the benefits of mountaineering, we believe this topic is on most hikers' list. From the moment mountaineering becomes part of your life, you will meet many people and create great friendships. Friends to walk, talk and have fun together.

IMPROVES MENTAL HEALTH

In an increasingly connected and pressure-filled world, whether at work or in personal life, practicing outdoor activities, breathing fresh air and connecting with nature has been the choice of many people.

In this sense, the practice of outdoor sports – such as mountaineering – brings more lightness and improves mental health.

It is important to note that mountaineering does not replace treatment with a qualified professional when necessary. However, the benefits of mountaineering for the physical and mental health of practitioners are undeniable.

INCREASES SELF-ESTEEM

Mountaineering increases self-esteem. This is because the practice of physical activity releases endorphins, the happiness hormone. In addition, contact with nature, with green, is therapeutic and you can connect with your essence. Finally, contact with the sun during walks activates vitamin D, improving calcium absorption, reducing the risk of diseases and helping with mood.

We know that this list is endless, but today we bring you the top five benefits of mountaineering for health and life.
